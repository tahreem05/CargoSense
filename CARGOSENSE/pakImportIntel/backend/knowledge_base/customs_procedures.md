# Customs Clearance Steps in Pakistan

1. **Filing of Goods Declaration (GD):**
   The importer or their clearing agent files the GD electronically through the WeBOC (Web Based One Customs) system.

2. **Assessment of Duties and Taxes:**
   The customs authorities assess the declared value and HS codes to calculate applicable Customs Duty (CD), Regulatory Duty (RD), Sales Tax (ST), and Withholding Tax (WHT).

3. **Examination:**
   Goods may be selected for physical examination (Red channel) or cleared through document review (Yellow channel) or automatically (Green channel).

4. **Payment:**
   The assessed duties and taxes are paid in the designated bank.

5. **Out of Charge:**
   After payment and satisfactory examination, customs issues an 'Out of Charge' order, allowing the goods to leave the port.
