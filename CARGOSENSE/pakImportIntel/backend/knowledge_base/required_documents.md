# Required Documents for Import into Pakistan

To successfully clear goods through Pakistan Customs, the following documents are mandatory:

1. **Bill of Lading (B/L) / Airway Bill (AWB):** Proof of shipment and ownership of goods.
2. **Commercial Invoice:** Detailed invoice from the supplier stating value, quantity, and description of goods.
3. **Packing List:** Detailed list of contents for each package/container.
4. **Certificate of Origin:** Required to claim preferential tariff rates under FTAs/PTAs.
5. **Letter of Credit (L/C) or E-Form:** Proof of authorized payment channel via State Bank of Pakistan.
6. **Insurance Certificate:** Proof that the goods are insured.
7. **NOCs/Certificates:** Specific items may require No Objection Certificates from bodies like the Ministry of Health, PTA, or PSQCA.
