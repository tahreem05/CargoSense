# Freight Forwarder Guide: How to Choose a Partner

A freight forwarder acts as your logistics intermediary, handling shipping, documentation, and sometimes customs clearance.

1. **What They Do:**
   - Book cargo space on ships or planes.
   - Arrange inland transportation.
   - Handle export/import paperwork.

2. **How to Choose:**
   - **Experience with Pakistan:** Ensure they have local agents in Karachi/Port Qasim.
   - **Specialization:** Some forwarders specialize in electronics, others in textiles. Choose one that knows your cargo type.
   - **Transparency:** Ask for an all-in quote breakdown (freight, THCs, documentation fees) to avoid hidden charges.

3. **Freight Forwarder vs. Clearing Agent:**
   The forwarder moves the goods to the port. The clearing agent (customs broker) handles the government paperwork to get it out of the port. Many large companies offer both services.
