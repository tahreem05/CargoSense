# Port Information: Key Entry Points in Pakistan

1. **Karachi Port (KPT):**
   - **Location:** Karachi, Sindh
   - **Characteristics:** The oldest and busiest port. Handles the majority of general cargo and containers. Often experiences high congestion.
   - **Code:** PKKAR

2. **Port Qasim (PQA):**
   - **Location:** 35km east of Karachi
   - **Characteristics:** Pakistan's second-busiest port. Specializes in bulk cargo, LNG, and has dedicated container terminals. Generally less congested than KPT.
   - **Code:** PKPQQ

3. **Gwadar Port:**
   - **Location:** Balochistan
   - **Characteristics:** A deep-water port, central to CPEC. Developing rapidly but currently handles lower volumes compared to Karachi ports.

4. **Torkham & Chaman:**
   - **Location:** KPK & Balochistan borders with Afghanistan
   - **Characteristics:** Key dry ports for land-based regional trade.
