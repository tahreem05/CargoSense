# Common Delay Reasons and Solutions

1. **Missing or Incomplete Documents:**
   - **Reason:** Missing Certificate of Origin, or inconsistencies between the invoice and B/L.
   - **Solution:** Always request draft documents from suppliers before the vessel sails to verify names, values, and HS codes.

2. **Customs Valuation Disputes:**
   - **Reason:** Customs believes you under-invoiced the goods to save on taxes.
   - **Solution:** Maintain proof of payment (TT/LC) and original correspondence. Be prepared for a valuation ruling.

3. **Port Congestion:**
   - **Reason:** High vessel traffic or lack of handling equipment at Karachi Port.
   - **Solution:** Build buffer time into your supply chain or route through Port Qasim if applicable.

4. **Regulatory Holds (NOCs):**
   - **Reason:** Importing chemicals, electronics, or food without prior approval.
   - **Solution:** Always check the FBR Import Policy Order for restricted items before ordering.
