# Import Policy 2024 Summary (Pakistan)

The Federal Board of Revenue (FBR) and Ministry of Commerce govern imports through the Import Policy Order.

**Key Highlights for 2024:**
1. **Banned Items:** Weapons, certain plastics, non-halal items, and items originating from specific embargoed nations are strictly banned.
2. **Restricted Items:** Some items require prior permission, such as pharmaceutical raw materials, telecom equipment (requires PTA NOC), and used vehicles.
3. **Foreign Exchange Regulations:** The State Bank of Pakistan tightly regulates outward remittances. Importers must use authorized banking channels (Letters of Credit, Registered Contracts) to pay suppliers.
4. **SRO Notifications:** The government frequently issues Statutory Regulatory Orders (SROs) that grant temporary duty exemptions or impose new taxes on specific sectors. Always check the latest active SROs.
