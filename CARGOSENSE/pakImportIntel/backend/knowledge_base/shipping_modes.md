# Shipping Modes: Air vs Sea, FCL vs LCL

1. **Sea Freight:**
   - **Best for:** Large, heavy, or non-urgent shipments.
   - **Cost:** Significantly cheaper than air freight.
   - **Transit Time:** 15-40 days depending on origin.
   - **Types:**
     - **FCL (Full Container Load):** You rent the entire container (20ft or 40ft). Faster clearance and less risk of damage.
     - **LCL (Less than Container Load):** Your goods share a container with others. Cheaper for small volumes but slower due to consolidation/deconsolidation.

2. **Air Freight:**
   - **Best for:** High-value, perishable, or extremely urgent items.
   - **Cost:** Expensive, charged by dimensional weight.
   - **Transit Time:** 1-5 days globally.
   - **Types:** General Cargo or Express Courier (DHL/FedEx).
